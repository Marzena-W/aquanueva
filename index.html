Client: Aqua Nueva
Tools: Figma, After Effects, Photoshop

Brief:
I was tasked to create print and digital assets for a Summer Terrace campaign introduicing collaboration between Aqua Nueva and Casamigos Tequila. The brief was to design a newsletter, a series of Instagram stories, and a menu.

Solution:
The idea aims to blend the distinct identities of both brands, to create a design that grabs attention.

Taking inspiration from Aqua, I integrated the White Heart font, which gives a sense of sophistication. To further elevate the design, I made use of the black and gold colour combination, adding a feeling of elegance. To add a touch of playfulness, I used turquoise inspired by Casamigos.

The summer cocktail menu is centred around the tequila selection. From classics to innovative mixes, each drink is crafted to showcase the unique flavours of Casamigos tequila.

To show the atmosphere of the terrace and highlight the partnership with Casamigos, I created a series of Instagram stories and newsletters. These visuals not only showcase the ambience of the terrace but also feature the range of Casamigos products.
